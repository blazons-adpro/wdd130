Overview

Purpose

This website is design to help producers connect and get their product to their customer locally and internationally, Consumers or customers can place orders which will be delivered to them at ease. It gives customers the opportunity to place orders for product and aslo gives sellers the opportunity to accept orders from customers without keeping stocks at hand, you won't have to leave the comfort of your home to go for orders but that can be done with your phone. With this website shopping becomes easier and faster for online buyers.

Audience

All business owners, companies both local and international who want to get their product faster to their customers through online orders and customers with interest and purchasing intentions. CAn be accessed by both young and old, educated and non educated.
